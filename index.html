About Us

A club built for confidence, character, and communication.

Who we are

Triumph Gavel Club is a Toastmasters Gavel Club dedicated to helping young people become confident communicators and future leaders. We create a supportive environment where members learn to speak, listen, lead, evaluate, and grow.

Our mission

To empower young people with confidence, communication skills, leadership qualities, and lifelong personal growth.

Our vision

To become a leading youth public speaking and leadership club in Mauritius, inspiring the next generation of confident leaders.
